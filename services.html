Our Services
It is a major concern at Precedent Dental Lab to provide our clients with the very best in service. Our customer care department is staffed by highly qualified and experienced personnel, to ensure that our dental lab customers get everything they need, quickly and efficiently. Over the years of providing products and services to the dental industry, we have recognized that one of the key challenges facing practitioners is the risk involved when they outsource the design and fabrication of restorations. Such risks to our clients are why we insist on maintaining the highest standards in everything we do. This intense emphasis on quality control begins with customer service and continues throughout the design and fabrication process. The core of this approach is to tailor all our services and products to the specific needs and preferences of individual dental professionals. By emphasizing such diversity and flexibility, we are able to expertly serve a wide range of dental specialists throughout the country
